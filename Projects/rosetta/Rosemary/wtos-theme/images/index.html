ABOUT rosette

rosette was established in 2002 at The Mall, Wood Green in North London. We specialise in men's designer jeans, shirts, jackets, t-shirt, trainers, belts, bags and accessories. Being one of the best independent retailer in designer menswear, we are always under pressure to create the trends first. We guarantee you will receive 100% authentic designer merchandise from all the big labels such as Franklin & Marshall, Hugo Boss, Stone Island, Polo Ralph Lauren, Money Clothing, Criminal Damage, Voi Jeans, Diesel, Vivienne Westwood etc.
