Structure client, appointment and property management

New Net SoftwareOne is one of the most feature rich sales and lettings software packages on the market. Tried and trusted by over 20,000 users since its launch, it helps to increase profit margins through a structured approach to managing clients, appointments, and properties. A desktop product with mobile capability for when you're on the road, it is more than a contact management database with reporting functionality. You can produce your own particulars, run email and social media marketing campaigns, as well as advertise in local papers, on your website and through property portals. All this functionality and more, allows you to keep your business in-house and your costs minimal.

Manage busy workloads

With the tools to provide full client management, New Net SoftwareOne allows you to stay in control over a busy workload that may well include vendors, landlords, applicants and tenants. As well as logging viewings, valuations and telephone calls, you can send vendor and landlord reports, emails, marketing and SMS. Automatic reminders and online client access mean you don't miss a trick and customers are always aware of progress.

Marketing your assets through our estate agent software couldn't be simpler. Property details only need to be entered once and will automatically be sent to your live web feed and portals. In one click, the data can be used for brochures and window cards, mail outs and emails to ensure your portfolio has full exposure.

Hit growth targets

Advanced users set up their own dashboards to report on all aspects of their agency, so staff better manage their targets and managers have a clear understanding of their pipeline and position in the market at any given time. The majority, start with our monthly and morning meeting reports for an overview and drill down for detailed statistics if required.
